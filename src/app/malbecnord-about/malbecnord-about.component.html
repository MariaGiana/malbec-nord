About Us

Established in 2025 in Copenhagen, Denmark, we are dedicated to introducing the world to the exceptional wines of Argentina.

Born from a deep appreciation for Argentine winemaking, our company focuses on curating and importing premium wines from across Argentina's most iconic regions—from Mendoza’s high-altitude vineyards to the unique terroirs of Patagonia and Salta.

Our mission is to bring not just wine, but a piece of Argentina’s soul to every glass—sharing culture, passion, and tradition with every bottle we deliver.
